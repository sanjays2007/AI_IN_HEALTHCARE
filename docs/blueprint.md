# **App Name**: Silent Guardian

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Risk Assessment: Analyzes patient data to predict dropout risk, providing a risk score and breakdown of contributing factors (financial, emotional, etc.). This tool predicts dropout probability.
- Behavioral Timeline Visualization: Presents a chronological view of patient behaviors (missed appointments, payment delays, side effect reports) to identify patterns.
- AI Recommendation Engine: Suggests personalized interventions based on the patient's risk profile, such as adjusting dosage, scheduling tele-consults, or referring to financial aid. This tool recommends personalized interventions.
- Intervention Logging and Tracking: Allows medical staff to record interventions performed and track their impact on the patient's risk score.
- Financial Aid Matching: Connects at-risk patients with eligible financial aid programs and government schemes.
- Mobile Patient Portal: Provides patients with access to their treatment plan, appointment scheduling, medication tracking, and side effect reporting via a mobile app.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm blue (#64B5F6) to convey trust and stability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F8) to provide a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft green (#A5D6A7) to highlight positive actions and interventions.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for clarity and modern feel.
- Use clear, intuitive icons to represent different risk factors and intervention types.
- Prioritize key information and use a card-based layout to improve readability and scannability.
- Subtle animations to indicate progress and success in treatment adherence.
